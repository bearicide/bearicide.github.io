what packages can include

  • Brand identity: logo direction, colors, type, visual rules, and basic usage guidance.
  • Website design: mobile-first pages, sections, navigation, links, and contact flow.
  • Promo design: flyers, posts, offers, announcements, launch graphics, or print-ready pieces.
  • Strategy + direction: messaging, structure, priorities, project order, and next steps.
  • Interactive builds: calculators, widgets, maps, demos, games, or custom page features.

custom can vary

A basic site is not the same job as an upgraded Choppa-style app with backend features, accounts, audio tools, hosting setup, or domain problems. Those pieces get scoped separately so nobody has to guess what the bill means.

bundle pricing

Bundle pricing means the listed work is grouped into one clear base price. The package price covers the defined deliverables, not every possible future idea humans can generate at 1:14 AM.

custom scope

Custom scope means extra work is priced by what it actually requires: pages, graphics, writing help, features, launch timing, backend work, unavailable domains, cleanup, or existing mess repair.

connected deliverables

Connected deliverables means the pieces are built to work together. The website, promo graphics, brand direction, links, and messaging should feel like one project, not five unrelated things taped together by panic.
