what packages can include

  • Brand identity: logo direction, colors, type, visual rules, and basic usage guidance.
  • Website design: mobile-first pages, sections, navigation, links, and contact flow.
  • Promo design: flyers, posts, offers, announcements, launch graphics, or print-ready pieces.
  • Strategy + direction: messaging, structure, priorities, project order, and next steps.
  • Interactive builds: calculators, widgets, maps, demos, games, or custom page features.
