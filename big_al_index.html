Family History

Legend says Big Al married into a certain confectionary dynasty. While the brother-in-law was busy with chocolate rivers and contract clauses, Big Al perfected smash patties and chili geometry. Then some duck-blooded relatives popped out of a portal with a spatula. The rest is deliciously complicated.
