The Choppa

Upload a loop or load the demo, chop to 16 pads, play by touch, QWERTY, or Launchkey Mini pads. Knobs map to FX. No learn mode. No apologetic science-fair sadness.

16 Pad Chop Grid

Performance

Press Start Audio, then load a loop. Launchkey Mini MK3 pads are mapped bottom row C2-B2 and top row E2-D#3.
Top row: E2 40, F2 41, F#2 42, G2 43, C3 48, C#3 49, D3 50, D#3 51
Bottom row: C2 36, C#2 37, D2 38, D#2 39, G#2 44, A2 45, A#2 46, B2 47
QWERTY: 1-8 top row, Q-I bottom row.
Knobs: CC 21-28 = Volume, Pitch, Attack, Release, Filter, Delay, Feedback, Drive.
