FAQ

What is MATTBEAR?

The umbrella brand and launcher for connected creative projects: music, games, tools, community pages, cards, and experiments.

Where should new art go?

Art belongs in a public route when it has a purpose: cards in Trading Cards, cover art in Music, reel assets in WORDPLAY, and map art in Interactive.

What is the next build step?

Turn static asset routes into working tools: playable WORDPLAY reels, interactive map states, card downloads, and music player hooks.
