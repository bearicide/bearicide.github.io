cleanup rules

  • Main stays the front door. It links out; it does not absorb every experiment.
  • Examples stays client-safe. Clear proof, plain labels, strong first impression.
  • Projects stays the map. No buried routes, mystery buttons, or duplicate public doors.
  • Games, music, and lab stay separated. Weird is fine. Confusing is expensive.
  • Every page needs the basics: purpose, home link, related route, mobile spacing, footer.
