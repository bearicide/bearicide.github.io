cleanup doctrine

  • Do not add new repos unless the route cannot live inside an existing district.
  • Every public page needs: clear purpose, home link, related route, readable mobile layout, and footer.
  • Archive duplicates after the best public version is selected.
  • Use shared assets before generating another pile of beautiful nonsense.
