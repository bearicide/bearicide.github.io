Word$lots quick guide

Spin manually or use Auto. Bonus and Rewind splashes pause play until cleared. Tap Spin to continue.

  • Spin: tap Spin or press Space. Press Space during a spin to fast-stop without changing the result.
  • Auto: A starts auto spin. S stops it.
  • Words: Only WORD and PLAY score, only as exact 4-letter payline hits. No 3-letter triggers. Wilds do not count.
  • Rewind: 3 cassettes trigger reverse spins at 84% RTP. Retriggering upgrades Full Reverse at 84% RTP.
  • Audio: starts quieter by default.
