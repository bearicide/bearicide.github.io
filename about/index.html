Chaos into signal.

I’m Matt Baier, building under MATTBEAR: part designer, part cook, part systems goblin, part neon bear trying to turn noisy real life into websites, games, music tools, branding systems, and useful digital weirdness.

Bearicide Productions is the workshop. HiberNation is the ecosystem. MATTBEAR is the creature at the front door yelling that the buttons should work, the page should load on a phone, and the whole thing should feel alive.

Technology is quietly human. I am loudly not.
